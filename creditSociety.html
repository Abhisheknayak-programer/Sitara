Credit Society

A comprehensive software solution for Credit co-operative societies

Cr-Soft is Software solution for Banking Transactions and Report Processing in Credit Cooperative Societies. This software Covers entire life cycle of a Deposit management right Like: Banking Management, File Processing and Report Generation, Amount Calculation, Processing of Accounts like FD, RD, Gold Loan and Personal loan etc. Cr-Soft Credit Co-operative Society is mainly working for a group of customers with Specific framed rules and Regulations. Crsoft caters to not just PACS, but all other sectors/categories of co-operative federation namely Consumer Credit sector, Fisheries, Multi-purpose societies, Milk producers federation, Sugar Federation, Tea-rubber growers, Textile co-operatives, School union societies, Hospital, Labour Union credit societies, Honey Manufactures societies etc

Adding Member and Apply Different type of loans and Approval for member to join Society through Board meetings and Member Account Managements.

  1. Report export facility to various useful formats such as MS word, Ms Excel, PDF etc.
  2. Extremely Functional and user friendly with user drivers operation. Attractive and well said reports format.
  3. For all types of loans -automatic loan interest and penal Interest calculation Facility as per society requirements.
  4. Entire application Works based on Customer number for easy transactions.
  5. For all transaction made, the SMS and email alert will be sent to the registered mobile number and registered mail-id.
  6. Additional amount concept for FD renewal process.
  7. Multiple disbursement of amount in term loan
  8. Bio metric authentication is being added as one of the security concerns as Login Process.
  9. Audit Report can be viewed and exported both in English and Kannada format.
