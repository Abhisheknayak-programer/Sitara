Data Migration

The process covers Pre-migration and post migration assurance. All programs, logics built for verification will be as per banks requirement.

The data migration process followed by Integrity of Data covering and not limited to confirmation that the data is not altered manually or electronically by a person/program and substitution or overwriting/transposition/ transcription in the migrated system

Review back up procedure so as to ensure The System Integrator will provide assurance of availability of data under conversion ensuring that and not limited to that the data is backed up before migration for future reference or any emergency that may arise out of data migration process.

Perform recurring gap analysis and ensure that all the gaps/discrepancies identified in the ‘Migration Audit Report’ are rectified by System Administrator. The gap analysis may require to be repeated until all errors identified are closed. We provide a ‘Final Compliance Report’ to certify the quality of data, efficiency of data migration process and stability of the data environment. To carry out two rounds of data migration verification pre migration and one round post migration of data

Final Compliance Report for a given implementation milestone shall be submitted of the System Integrator confirming that gaps mentioned in the ‘Migration Audit Report’ have been corrected.
